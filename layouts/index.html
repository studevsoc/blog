Student Developers Society

We are a community with a vision to bridge the gap between students and professionals. Our mission is to make proper education accessible to last person on farthest corner of the planet. We believe that access to proper education is the right of every human and we work tirelessly to make education more accessible to more people everyday.

About Us

Things We Do

Skill Enhancement

Join in on our workshops, talks, interactive sessions and a lot more to pick up a new skill or to enhance your skill through our well educated instructors.

Meetups

Get involved in meetups of a wonderful community of developers and aspiring developers to share ideas and to know about the best practices in the development world.

Software Developement

We are advocates of Free/Libre Software. We are a community of makers and are constantly working on various fun projects.

Networking

Participate in meetups and workshops conducted in association with different communities to get to know interesting people while picking up new skills along the way.

A few things we did that we're particularly proud of.

Rails Girls Kochi

March 11, 2018 | KTIZ Kalamassery

Rails Girls Workshop is a global movement/event organized across the world to encourage more girls to choose and stay in a career of software development. Rails Girls Kochi was organized by Kerala Ruby User Group and Student Developer Society. It was the first major event organized by SDS.

Debutsav Kochi

November 24, 2018 | School of Engineering, Cochin University of Science and Technology, Kalamassery, Ernakulam, Kerala.

MiniDebConfs are local meetings organized by Debian project members to achieve similar objectives to those of the DebConf, but on a regional context. When majority of talks are not Debian specific, it is called DebUtsav - celebration of Free Software by Debian community. Debutsav was organized by Free Software Community of India, Democratic Alliance for Knowledge Freedom (DAKF) and Student Developer Society.

Designer Fest

April 13, 2019 | InQ Innovation Kochi

Designer fest was the first of it's kind designer oriented meetup to happen in Kochi. It bought discussions regarding design concepts and the use of FOSS tools for professional design needs. It was the first non developer oriented event organized by student developer society.

LetsPy 2019

November 02-03 2019 | Rajiv Gandhi Institute Of Technology (Government Engineering College, Kottayam)

LetsPy was a two day Django Python ‘workathon’ (workshop/hackathon) aimed at introducing novice enthusiasts/students to the world of computer science and collaborative development. This event is organized by four leading communities of Kerala. Student Developer Society, Kochi Python, Tinkerhub and Pehia Foundation.
